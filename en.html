About Us

Crafting Precision
Fasteners Since 1987

Founded in 1987 in Yueqing, Zhejiang — China's fastener capital — Tianhong specialises in R&D and manufacturing of high-strength bolts, nuts, washers and custom fasteners for automotive, solar PV and industrial machinery applications worldwide.

Leveraging advanced cold-forging, CNC machining and rigorous quality control, we deliver reliable fastener solutions to clients in 50+ countries, driven by customer requirements and continuous innovation.
