关于我们About Us

深耕紧固件制造
超过三十五年 Crafting Precision
Fasteners Since 1987

浙江天虹紧固件有限公司成立于1987年，位于中国紧固件之乡——浙江乐清。公司专注于高强度螺栓、螺母、垫圈及各类异型紧固件的研发与生产，产品广泛应用于汽车零部件、光伏支架系统、工业机械设备等领域。 Founded in 1987 in Yueqing, Zhejiang — China's fastener capital — Tianhong specialises in R&D and manufacturing of high-strength bolts, nuts, washers and custom fasteners for automotive, solar PV and industrial machinery applications worldwide.

依托先进的冷镦成型、CNC精密加工及严格的质量管控体系，我们为全球50余个国家和地区的客户提供可靠的紧固件解决方案。 Leveraging advanced cold-forging, CNC machining and rigorous quality control, we deliver reliable fastener solutions to clients in 50+ countries, driven by customer requirements and continuous innovation.

精密检测Precision Testing
