About Us

Leading Fastener Manufacturer Since 1987

tianhong (formerly Yueqing Tianhong Special Fastener Factory) was founded in 1987 and has grown to become one of the most reputable fastener manufacturers in Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province.

With over 35 years of experience in fastener manufacturing, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for businesses worldwide, delivering high-quality fastening solutions across multiple industries.
